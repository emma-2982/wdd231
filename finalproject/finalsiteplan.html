Jamila's Cake House — Site Plan

Site Name

Jamila's Cake House

This name captures the warmth and creativity of Jamila's bakery, known for custom cakes and sweet treats. It's friendly, memorable, and evokes a sense of home and celebration.

Site Purpose

The website will showcase Jamila’s delicious cakes and pastries, provide an online ordering option, display a photo gallery of creations, and share contact details for custom orders. It aims to attract both local customers and event planners seeking personalized desserts.

Scenarios

  • “Can I order a custom birthday cake online?”
  • “What flavors and designs are available for wedding cakes?”
  • “Where is Jamila's Cake House located and how can I contact them?”

Color Scheme

These colors will be used throughout the site:

Primary Color — Chocolate Brown (#D2691E)

Used for: headings, navigation bar, footer background.

Accent Color — Soft Pink (#F8C8DC)

Used for: buttons, highlights, and decorative accents.

Typography

  • Playfair Display — headings and titles (elegant, bakery-style)
  • Open Sans — body text (clean and readable)
